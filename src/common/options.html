What is PSChrome

PSChrome is a browser extension/add-on that works with both Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox. PSChrome adds usability features to enhance the user experience of both technical and functional users. By adding visual tweeks and automating commons tasks, PSChrome will be a resource you won't want to live without.
